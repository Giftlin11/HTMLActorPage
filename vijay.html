Vijay

About vijay:Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, known mononymously as Vijay, is a prominent Indian actor, dancer, playback singer, and philanthropist, primarily working in Tamil cinema. Early Life and Background Birth: Vijay was born on June 22, 1974, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. He is the son of film director S. A. Chandrasekhar and playback singer Shoba Chandrasekhar. He had a sister named Vidhya, who passed away at a young age. Education: He attended Loyola College in Chennai but dropped out to pursue his acting career. He made his acting debut as a child artist in the film Vetri (1984) directed by his father. Career Highlights Acting Career: Vijay made his lead role debut in Naalaiya Theerpu (1992) at the age of 18. He gained popularity with films like Poove Unakkaga (1996) and Kadhalukku Mariyadhai (1997). Over the years, he has starred in numerous successful films, including Ghilli (2004), Pokkiri (2007), Thuppakki (2012), Mersal (2017), Sarkar (2018), and Master (2021). 2 Awards and Recognition: Vijay is one of the highest-paid actors in Tamil cinema and has received several awards, including eight Vijay Awards and three Tamil Nadu State Film Awards.
